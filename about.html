Our Services

BODY BUILDING

A combination of cardio exercises, strength and endurance exercises. The first part is dynamic with fast cardio exercises, the second is static with cardio exercises.

Each workout is done next to another muscle group. Emphasis is placed on the muscles of the abdomen, legs, gluteus, back, arms and shoulders

YOGA EXERCISE

This is a great program of exercises for achieving fitness, but also for mental and emotional calm. The technique of breathing, which is an energy skill, is mastered.

BOXING

Fall in love with our boxing and kickboxing classes—a mind-body challenge made to transform and uplift.

CROSSFIT

CrossFit workouts are different every day and can be modified to help each athlete achieve their goals.

BPILATES FITNESS

The Pilates method is not just an exercise, it is a series of controlled movements that engage your body and mind.
